Welcome to Caldwell Astronomy Club

Join us in exploring the mysteries of the cosmos. Our club is dedicated to stargazing, learning about celestial objects, and sharing our passion for astronomy.

About Us

The Caldwell Astronomy Club was founded in 2023 with the goal of bringing together astronomy enthusiasts of all levels. We organize regular stargazing events, educational talks, and workshops.
